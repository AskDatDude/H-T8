    For accessibility reasons, this guide is written in English. If you need help with the Finnish language, please ask for assistance on Discord.

    This guide is designed to help you get started with hacking, and understanding the basic concepts. It is not a comprehensive guide, but rather a starting point for your journey into the world of hacking.

    By compleating this guide, you will have the mimimum knowledge required to participate in the club's activities.

    The guide is divided into several sections, each covering a different topic.

    The sections are designed to be completed in order, but you can also skip to the sections that interest you the most.

    The guide is not a step-by-step tutorial, but rather a collection of resources and information that will help you learn the basics of hacking.

    You do not need to complete the entire guide in one sitting, you can take your time and come back to it later. The progress you make will be saved, so you can pick up where you left off.
